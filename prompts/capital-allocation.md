# Capital Allocation Prompt

Goal: Explain how management allocates capital.

Inputs:
- 10-K MD&A and cash flow statements.
- Debt, buyback, and dividend history.

Instructions:
- Summarize reinvestment, M&A, debt repayment, and shareholder returns.
- Evaluate consistency with stated strategy.
- Cite sources and log them in `docs/source-log.md`.
