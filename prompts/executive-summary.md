# Executive Summary Prompt

Goal: Write a concise, decision-oriented summary (5-7 bullet points).

Inputs:
- Latest report metrics and valuation outputs.
- Key findings from 10-K and transcripts.

Instructions:
- Lead with the investment thesis and the biggest risk.
- Include current price vs. base-case value and margin of safety.
- Highlight leverage and cash generation quality.
- Cite sources in-line and log them in `docs/source-log.md`.
