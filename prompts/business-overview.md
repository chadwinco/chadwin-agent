# Business Overview Prompt

Goal: Describe what the company does, how it makes money, and key segments.

Inputs:
- `companies/<TICKER>/data/10-K-*.md`
- `companies/<TICKER>/data/company_profile.csv`

Instructions:
- Summarize products/services, customer types, and geographies.
- Note any major subsidiaries or brands.
- Keep to 2-3 short paragraphs.
- Cite sources and log them in `docs/source-log.md`.
