# Competitive Position Prompt

Goal: Explain the moat (or lack thereof) and why returns are sustainable.

Inputs:
- 10-K business and risk sections.
- Industry sources and competitors.

Instructions:
- Identify structural advantages: scale, network effects, switching costs, regulation, brand.
- Compare to key peers on margins and returns.
- Use evidence. Avoid generic statements.
- Cite sources and log them in `docs/source-log.md`.
