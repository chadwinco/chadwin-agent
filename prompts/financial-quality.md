# Financial Quality Prompt

Goal: Assess earnings quality, margins, and capital efficiency.

Inputs:
- `companies/<TICKER>/data/financials/annual/*.csv`.
- `docs/data-dictionary.md` for metric definitions.

Instructions:
- Comment on revenue growth trend, margin stability, and FCF conversion.
- Discuss ROIC vs. cost of capital and what drives it.
- Note leverage and liquidity risks.
- Cite sources and log them in `docs/source-log.md`.
