## Business Overview
Levi Strauss & Co operates in the Apparel Manufacturing space within the Consumer Discretionary sector. The company is headquartered in San Francisco, CA and reports in USD.

## Competitive Position
Over the last four fiscal years, average EBIT margin was 6.9%, FCF margin 5.7%, and ROIC 16.4%. These economics provide a data-backed view of competitive strength, but still require qualitative validation.

## Financial Quality
Key metrics show average revenue growth of 7.7%, EBIT margin of 6.9%, FCF margin of 5.7%, and ROIC of 16.4% over the last four fiscal years. Net debt to EBITDA averages 0.7x, providing a view of balance-sheet leverage.

## Capital Allocation
Free cash flow averaged 5.7% of revenue over the last four fiscal years, with reinvestment running at 3.7%. Net debt increased by $0.2b over the same period, indicating how much of cash generation has gone to balance-sheet management.

## Growth Opportunities
- Organic net revenue growth -- 7% growth for the fiscal year, with fiscal Q4 up 5% compared to a prior-year increase of 8%, and November holiday season up 7% (highest holiday revenue in at least a decade).
- Levi's brand performance -- 7% growth in fiscal 2025, remaining the top denim brand globally and holding more share than the next two largest competitors combined.
- Direct-to-consumer (DTC) transformation -- DTC channel grew 11%, now representing approximately half of total business; fiscal Q4 DTC sales rose 10%, marking the fifteenth straight quarter of positive comps.
- DTC margin expansion -- Significant margin expansion delivered in DTC for fiscal 2025, supported by high single-digit comp growth and operational improvements in stores and e-commerce.
