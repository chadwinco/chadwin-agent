# Abercrombie & Fitch Co. (ANF) Deep-Dive Revision

_As of 2026-02-12 (Baseline: 2026-02-12)_

## Executive Delta
- Prior verdict: Attractive
- Revised verdict: Watch
- Prior base value: $129.54/share
- Revised base value: $99.23/share
- Prior current price: $91.48
- Revised current price input: $91.48
- Base MOS change: +41.6% to +8.5% (down 33.1 pts)

The deep dive changed the conclusion mainly on margin durability.

Key decision-changing findings:
- Q3 FY2025 margin pressure was large and quantifiable: cost of sales rate worsened ~260 bps y/y, including ~210 bps adverse tariff impact; YTD COGS rate worsened ~300 bps with ~90 bps tariff impact and ~40 bps freight headwind. (source: companies/US/ANF/data/filings/10-Q-2025-12-05-0001018840-25-000048.md)
- Brand mix concentration increased: Q3 net sales were -2% for Abercrombie versus +16% for Hollister; YTD was -4% versus +19%, reducing confidence in blended growth durability. (source: companies/US/ANF/data/filings/10-Q-2025-12-05-0001018840-25-000048.md)
- APAC remains the weakest region with a Q3 net sales decline and deeper segment losses year to date, limiting international upside quality. (source: companies/US/ANF/data/filings/10-Q-2025-12-05-0001018840-25-000048.md)
- External checks were mixed: retail data stayed positive, but peer disclosures still showed tariff and inventory/markdown pressure, supporting more conservative margin assumptions. (source: companies/US/ANF/reports/2026-02-12/third-party-sources.md)

## Residual Risks and Monitoring Signals
- Tariff pass-through failure: if 2026 pricing and sourcing mitigation does not offset cost pressure, margin normalization could undershoot base case. Disconfirming signal: sustained gross margin and operating margin stabilization without elevated promotion. (sources: companies/US/ANF/data/filings/earnings-call-2026-01-22-news.alphastreet.com.md, companies/US/ANF/data/filings/10-Q-2025-12-05-0001018840-25-000048.md)
- Brand concentration risk: if Hollister decelerates before Abercrombie returns to consistent growth, consolidated growth could fall toward bear path. Disconfirming signal: Abercrombie comp trend moving positive for multiple quarters. (source: companies/US/ANF/data/filings/10-Q-2025-12-05-0001018840-25-000048.md)
- APAC structural drag: continued negative APAC economics could require prolonged investment or further impairments. Disconfirming signal: APAC operating loss rate narrowing with improving comparable sales. (sources: companies/US/ANF/data/filings/10-Q-2025-12-05-0001018840-25-000048.md, companies/US/ANF/data/filings/historical/10-Q-2025-09-05-0001018840-25-000045.md)
- Capital allocation tightening: ongoing large repurchases in volatile conditions could reduce optionality. Disconfirming signal: buyback pace moderated when free-cash conversion weakens. (source: companies/US/ANF/data/filings/10-Q-2025-12-05-0001018840-25-000048.md)

## Conclusion
ANF still screens as slightly undervalued in base case ($99.23 vs $91.48), but the deep dive reduced the margin of safety to single digits and increased confidence in downside asymmetry under execution or cost shocks. The name moves from Attractive to Watch until evidence shows durable margin recovery, less concentrated growth contribution, and clearer APAC improvement.
