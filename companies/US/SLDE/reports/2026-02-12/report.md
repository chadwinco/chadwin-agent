## Business and Competitive Position
Slide is a technology-enabled coastal specialty P&C insurer launched in 2021, writing homeowners and related residential products primarily through Slide Insurance Company, with distribution through independent agents and direct-to-consumer channels. (Source: companies/US/SLDE/data/filings/S-1-A-2025-06-09-0001193125-25-137410.md)

The strategic edge claimed by management is policy-level underwriting selection that explicitly incorporates prospective reinsurance cost and catastrophe risk, plus vertical integration across underwriting, claims, and risk operations. (Source: companies/US/SLDE/data/filings/S-1-A-2025-06-09-0001193125-25-137410.md)

## Financial Quality

### Key business levers driving valuation
- Underwriting margin durability (loss ratio + expense ratio discipline).
- Catastrophe season loss severity versus reinsurance structure.
- Citizens take-out and renewal pipeline quality.
- Regulatory capital flexibility and dividend capacity at the operating insurer.
(Sources: companies/US/SLDE/data/filings/10-Q-2025-11-06-0001193125-25-267339.md, companies/US/SLDE/data/filings/S-1-A-2025-06-09-0001193125-25-137410.md)

### Revenue growth by market
SLDE remains overwhelmingly Florida-driven: the September 2025 filing states Florida policyholders represented 99% of direct written premiums for the quarter, with South Carolina as a small secondary market. This concentration supports near-term scale in known markets but raises event-risk concentration. (Source: companies/US/SLDE/data/filings/10-Q-2025-11-06-0001193125-25-267339.md)

### Revenue growth by product line
Growth is driven mainly by residential premium expansion and policy growth: 9M 2025 gross premiums earned rose to $1,048.9M (from $773.1M), net premiums earned rose to $753.0M (from $567.8M), and policies in force reached 351,707 at September 30, 2025. (Source: companies/US/SLDE/data/filings/10-Q-2025-11-06-0001193125-25-267339.md)
