## Business and Competitive Position
NICE operates two reportable segments: Customer Engagement and Financial Crime and Compliance. The Customer Engagement business centers on CXone Mpower, while the Financial Crime and Compliance business focuses on embedded-AI anti-fraud/AML and market-compliance workflows. (Source: `companies/US/NICE/data/filings/20-F-2025-03-19-0001003935-25-000004.md`)

Economically, the key value driver is cloud subscription scale plus AI attach. Management disclosed Q3 cloud at 77% of revenue and highlighted rising AI/self-service ARR and cloud backlog growth. The annual filing also shows remaining performance obligations of about $3.07B, with the majority expected to be recognized over 24 months, supporting medium-term revenue visibility. (Source: `companies/US/NICE/data/filings/earnings-call-2025-11-14-insidermonkey.com.md`; `companies/US/NICE/data/filings/20-F-2025-03-19-0001003935-25-000004.md`)
