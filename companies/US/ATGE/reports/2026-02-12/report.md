## Business and Competitive Position
ATGE is a focused healthcare-education platform with three operating segments: Chamberlain, Walden, and Medical & Veterinary (AUC, RUSM, RUSVM). The current model combines high-volume U.S. nursing/professional programs with international medical/veterinary schools. [Source: /Users/chad/source/chadwin-codex/companies/US/ATGE/data/filings/10-Q-2026-01-28-0001104659-26-007415.md]

Enrollment momentum remains favorable in the latest filing window (Walden +13.0% year over year at December 31, 2025), while Chamberlain has shifted from rapid growth to a more mixed pattern by session. That mix dynamic matters for forward margins and marketing intensity. [Source: /Users/chad/source/chadwin-codex/companies/US/ATGE/data/filings/10-Q-2026-01-28-0001104659-26-007415.md]

The company also announced a corporate rename to Covista and a future ticker change to `CVSA` effective February 24, 2026, which does not change current economics but is relevant for monitoring continuity across filings/data feeds. [Source: /Users/chad/source/chadwin-codex/companies/US/ATGE/data/filings/8-K-2026-02-06-0001104659-26-011611.md]
