## Business and Competitive Position
BHF distributes annuity and life products through independent channels and reported over 2.2 million contracts/policies in force at year-end 2024. Core economics are anchored in spread and fee income from annuities, plus mortality/expense margins in life and run-off blocks. (Source: companies/US/BHF/data/filings/10-K-2025-02-28-0001685040-25-000010.md)

Competitive durability is moderate: BHF has meaningful scale in Shield annuities and broad distribution relationships, but returns are exposed to interest rates, hedging effectiveness, and policyholder behavior assumptions. Management’s own commentary highlights the need for capital discipline and reinsurance/hedging execution to protect RBC and earnings stability. (Sources: companies/US/BHF/data/filings/10-K-2025-02-28-0001685040-25-000010.md, companies/US/BHF/data/filings/earnings-call-2026-01-23-news.alphastreet.com.md)
