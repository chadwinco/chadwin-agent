#### Item 8.01. Other Events.
On May 19, 2025, Brighthouse Financial, Inc. (the “Company”) received notice from Potemkin Limited (“Potemkin”) of its unsolicited “mini-tender” offer to purchase up to 100,000 shares of the Company’s common stock at an offer price of $36.00 per share. The number of shares subject to Potemkin’s offer represents less than 0.2% of the Company’s common stock outstanding as of the date of the offer.

On May 20, 2025, the Company issued a news release, a copy of which is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1, informing its shareholders that the Company does not endorse Potemkin’s unsolicited mini-tender offer and recommending that shareholders do not tender their shares. The offer is at a price below the current market price for the Company’s common stock and which is an approximate 41.12% discount to the closing price of the Company’s common stock as of May 19, 2025 ($61.14 per share). Shareholders who tender their shares may withdraw them in the manner described in Potemkin’s offering documents. The tender offer is currently scheduled to expire at 5:00 p.m., New York City time, on September 16, 2025, unless extended or earlier revoked by Potemkin.

The Company is not affiliated or associated in any way with Potemkin, its mini-tender offer, or the offer documentation. Additional information concerning mini-tender offer is included in the news release attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and incorporated herein by reference.
