## Business and Competitive Position
ASML supplies semiconductor manufacturers with hardware, software, and services used to produce integrated circuits at scale, and it positions lithography as central to process shrink and economics. The portfolio spans EUV and DUV platforms and associated process-control offerings, which supports broad participation across advanced and mainstream nodes. (source: `companies/ASML/data/filings/20-F-2025-03-05-0000937966-25-000009.md`)

Competitive strength is most visible at the leading edge: ASML discloses it is the sole manufacturer of EUV lithography systems and describes High-NA EUV ramp plans tied to customer roadmaps. At the same time, management describes the industry as cyclical and exposed to export-control and geopolitical friction, which can shift demand timing and customer capex behavior. (source: `companies/ASML/data/filings/20-F-2025-03-05-0000937966-25-000009.md`)

## Key Risks and Disconfirming Signals
- Customer concentration risk: ASML reports that four customers represented 53.8% of total net sales in 2024; a pause by any one major customer can materially affect near-term cash generation. Disconfirming signal: concentration falls while aggregate revenue and margin stay resilient. (source: `companies/ASML/data/filings/20-F-2025-03-05-0000937966-25-000009.md`)
- Regulatory/export-control risk: ASML highlights export controls and licensing complexity as factors that can affect delivery timing, serviceability, and market access. Disconfirming signal: stable shipment mix and no material disruptions tied to new controls. (source: `companies/ASML/data/filings/20-F-2025-03-05-0000937966-25-000009.md`)
- Semiconductor cycle risk: management explicitly characterizes the industry as cyclical with fab timing shifts and uneven customer benefit from AI demand. Disconfirming signal: sustained order and shipment growth through a full cycle without major push-outs. (source: `companies/ASML/data/filings/20-F-2025-03-05-0000937966-25-000009.md`)
- Supply chain concentration risk: ASML discloses reliance on single or limited suppliers for key components and subassemblies. Disconfirming signal: improved multi-sourcing and no throughput bottlenecks in critical modules. (source: `companies/ASML/data/filings/20-F-2025-03-05-0000937966-25-000009.md`)
- Valuation-expectation risk: the current price sits between this run's base and bull outcomes, so realized execution has less room for error than the bear case implies. Disconfirming signal: sustained conversion of AI and advanced-node demand into revenue and cash generation consistent with bull assumptions. (source: `companies/ASML/reports/2026-02-07/valuation/outputs.json`, `companies/ASML/data/company_profile.csv`)

At EUR 1,413.01 per share, the updated multi-stage base case is close but still below market, so the call shifts to Watch rather than Avoid. This framework better reflects a longer competitive-advantage period, but the entry point still leans on continued high execution versus the bear path. The fastest way to invalidate this cautious view is sustained delivery of growth and cash margins consistent with the bull scenario over the next several reporting periods.
