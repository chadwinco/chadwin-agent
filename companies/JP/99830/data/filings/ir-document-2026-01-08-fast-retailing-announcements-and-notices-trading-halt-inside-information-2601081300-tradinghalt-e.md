TRADING HALT  
  
At the request of Fast Retailing Co., Ltd. (the “Company”), trading in its Hong Kong depositary receipts 
on The Stock Exchange of Hong Kong Limited will be halted with effect from 1:00 p.m. on Thursday, 
January 8, 2026 , pending the release of the quarterly results announcement of the Company and its 
subsidiaries for the three months ended November 30, 2025, which constitutes inside information.
