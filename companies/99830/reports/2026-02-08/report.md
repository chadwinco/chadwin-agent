## Business and Competitive Position
Fast Retailing is a multi-brand apparel platform led by UNIQLO, with additional scale in GU and Theory. Its core operating model remains vertically integrated across design, procurement, production, and retail distribution under the LifeWear concept, which supports product consistency and global brand positioning. (Source: companies/99830/data/filings/ir-document-2024-12-31-ar2024-en-ar2024-en.md)

The company’s international footprint is now central to the thesis. In FY2024, UNIQLO International had 1,698 stores and management continues to prioritize Europe/North America expansion and quality-store rollout. The FY2026 Q1 results presentation also points to continuing double-digit growth expectations in key overseas markets, but management explicitly notes that actual outcomes can vary with demand conditions, price competition, and exchange-rate moves. (Source: companies/99830/data/filings/ir-document-2024-12-31-ar2024-en-ar2024-en.md; companies/99830/data/filings/ir-document-2026-01-08-fast-retailing-results-for-september-to-november-2025-and-estimates-for--20260108-results-en.md)

## Conclusion
At JPY 63,500, the market price sits materially above this run’s JPY 42,017 base intrinsic value and assumes execution that is closer to the bull path. Fast Retailing is a high-quality global operator with strong recent momentum, but the current valuation leaves limited room for demand or margin disappointment. I rate 99830 as Avoid at the current price and would revisit on either a better entry price or more evidence that elevated growth/margin durability can persist beyond the near-term cycle.
