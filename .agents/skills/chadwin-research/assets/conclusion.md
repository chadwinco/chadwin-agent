# Conclusion Prompt

Goal: End with a direct action recommendation at the current price.

Inputs:
- Investment summary, valuation table, and risk analysis.

Instructions:
- Write 3-4 sentences with a clear call: `Attractive`, `Watch`, or `Avoid`.
- Tie the call to valuation versus price and key uncertainty.
- State whether key investment questions are resolved to decision quality or still require follow-up.
- Include 1-2 follow-up checks that could invalidate the current view.
