| 2026-02-08 | Financials valuation fit | FCF-based DCF can misstate intrinsic value for insurers where reserves, hedging, and regulatory capital drive economics. | Added a residual-income pathway (book value + excess returns) to the research skill, updated workflow quality gates for financials, and applied it to LNC with explicit ROE/cost-of-equity assumptions. |
